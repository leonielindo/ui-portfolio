About me!

I am passionate about providing good user journeys and an intuitive user interface which is not only user centric but works with server side information to meet the stakeholder business needs. I enjoy the process of building web solutions that are scalable that adheres to W3C standards, along with incorporating semantic mark-up and accessibility practices.

I have extensive experience in responsive web development and enjoy being able to participate in the lifecycle of a project to help re-brand a product or a company, with the challenge of delivering good user journeys and intuitive user interface features to meet business needs

I also did a User Experience course at General Assembly which I enjoyed greatly and would like to utilise the knowledge and skills into my career as a UX/UI Developer.
